Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I have to be to enter?

This event is open to all students ages 13 through 18.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Make sure you bring these items:

  • A personal computer
  • Money for dinner cuz there's good food options in the area!
  • Comfortable clothes! (You'll be here for ~6 hours)
  • Media release / emergency contact forms

Are there any travel scholarships?

PDX MetroHacks supports students through partnering with HackClub's GasFund. Make sure to snap a selfie of yourself while you're here!
